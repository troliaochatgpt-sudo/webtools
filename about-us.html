Our Purpose

This website is part of my Launch Studio Web Development training. It serves as a central place where I practice building accessible, responsive, and well-structured pages using HTML, CSS, W3.CSS, and Flexbox.

Every page here reflects something I’ve learned — navigation, layout, forms, images, tables, and overall page organization.
